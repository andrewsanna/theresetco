Our Philosophy

Organizing for function, flow, and forgiveness.

At The Reset Co., we believe a clutter-free home isn't about discipline, perfection, or doing things "right." It's about creating systems that support the way you actually live.

Life is full — busy schedules, shifting seasons, and changing needs. Your home should meet you there, not make you feel behind. That's why we don't organize for aesthetics alone.

We design systems that:

  • Reduce daily decision fatigue
  • Absorb real life (not fight it)
  • Make it easy to reset — again and again

We don't believe in guilt-based organizing. If something isn't working, the solution isn't more effort — it's a better system. You deserve a space that feels calm, clear, and breathable — not just on good days, but on the hard ones too.

When life changes, your home can change with you. That's the reset.

Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Anna Andrews.

I've always been drawn to creating order out of chaos. For as long as I can remember, I was the friend who would happily reorganize a pantry or a closet, finding joy in giving everything a home.

Interior design has always inspired me, and even when I tried to put off other tasks, I'd inevitably end up reorganizing a room just for the calm and clarity it gave me.

My background is in real estate development, where I loved planning spaces that weren't just beautiful, but also efficient and easy to live in. That experience taught me the power of thoughtful design and how much the flow of a room impacts the way we feel day to day.

Since COVID, with both my husband and I working from home (and Cooper always nearby), I quickly learned how important it is to create a space that supports focus while still feeling like a retreat. Through that process, I experienced firsthand how a clean, well-planned environment doesn't just look better — it creates mental clarity, saves time, and truly changes the way you live in your space.

That's why I started this business: to help others experience the same peace and simplicity that comes from a home designed to work for you.
